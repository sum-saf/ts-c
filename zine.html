This is a zine that goes over voguing, from where it come from to the different voguing moves. "5 Elements" refers to the 5 elements of vogue: hands, catwalk, duckwalk, floor performance, and spins & dips. These are the moves signature to the voguing dance style. The zine below goes over what those moves are, the history behind them, and the different styles of vogue.
