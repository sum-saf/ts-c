"Category Is..." is a transmedia project that talks about ballroom culture. The title is in reference to ball events as there are different categories that houses compete against each other to win trophies, money, and other prizes.

Ballroom culture is a big part of the LGBT+ community, especially African American and Latinx. The ballroom was a safe space for those who weren't accepted in their families to be themselves. This project explores what ballroom culture is and what is it today.
